How It Works

Challenges: Every 6 hours for the first 24 hours, a new battle will be announced between Team Bonk and Team Pump. These challenges are crucial, as the team with the most wins will earn a boost (such as additional liquidity, burns, or airdrops), while the losing team will face a penalty (losing part of their supply). After the initial 24 hours, one challenge will take place every 24 hours for the remaining 16% of the supply.

Supply Locking: Both coins will start with a 20% locked supply, controlled by the dev team. Immediately, 16% of the supply will be locked for 17 days, with 1% unlocking every 24 hours to participate in each new challenge. During the first 24 hours, the supply will be locked with 4% remaining unlocked for the first four challenges.

The Goal: The ultimate goal is to empower the community. After a few days, the community will take control of the winnings and decide the next steps for both teams, including how the rewards are distributed or how the boost is used. This puts the power in the hands of the players, allowing them to shape the future of both Team Bonk and Team Pump.
